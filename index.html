Biography

I am a PhD candidate (2022–present) at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Angela Yao. As a member of the Computer Vision and Machine Learning Group, I focus on video understanding—especially 4D scene reconstruction and interpretation—to bridge visual perception and spatiotemporal reasoning in dynamic environments.

I am also a research intern at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (MPI‑IS), advised by Michael J. Black, where my work centers on 4D reconstruction of humans and scenes from video.
